About IVTD

Inventions Village Technical Design was founded by Aref Al Awadhi, an activist in inventions and product development, on October 12, 2024, in the Kingdom of Bahrain.

Mr. Al Awadhi is an active member and one of the founders of the Bahrain Researchers and Inventors Society (BRAINS). He also helped establish the Electronics Department at the Children’s and Youth Science Center, and has participated in exhibitions, scientific forums, and judging committees.

Books by Aref Al Awadhi

  • The Story of Electricity and Electronics: A Journey Through the History of Key Inventions.
  • How to Become an Inventor.
  • Invention: From Idea to Business (published in both Arabic and English).

Vision

Towards distinguished work in developing and investing in inventions based on creativity and innovation.

Mission

To strive to invest in inventions and innovations for the general benefit of society.

Services

Study technical problems & design solutions

Feasibility studies (technical, practical, commercial)

Consultations & assistance for inventors

Guidance on development steps, prototyping, and routes to market and partnerships.

Training courses

Workshops and programs to build skills for students, innovators, and teams.
